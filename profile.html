Our Mission

Pimath aims to make learning mathematics simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone. The app is designed to provide quick math practice with a clean and distraction-free experience.

About the Developer

Pimath is developed by an independent creator who is passionate about education and technology. The focus is on building lightweight, privacy-first applications that work seamlessly across platforms.

Design Philosophy

The app follows a minimal design approach inspired by iOS and Android UI principles. It avoids unnecessary features, ensuring users can focus purely on learning.
